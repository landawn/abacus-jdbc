NOTE
----
For a FULL-capability CrudLDao DAO the capability-split refactor (commit 8a3d8246) is
completely transparent at the method level: nothing was added, removed, renamed, or had
its signature changed. What the refactor changed is only WHERE each method is *declared*
(the same 230 methods were redistributed across new capability interfaces), not the
callable surface:

The surface-changing effect of the refactor appears only on the RESTRICTED variants
(ReadOnlyCrudLDao: 230 -> 169; NoUpdateCrudLDao: 230 -> 196) -- see their reports. The 25
primitive-`long` read overloads now live in CrudLReadOps (shared by all three checked
Long CRUD variants), which is why CrudLDao's own declared count drops from 28 to 3.
