1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
OP is an enum of execution modes consumed by AbstractQuery and @Query. The constants map cleanly to the
AbstractQuery terminal-op family and each is well documented with a correct @see. It has no methods. Two
minor items remain: one casing inconsistency (DEFAULT) and two already-deprecated constants (query, stream).
