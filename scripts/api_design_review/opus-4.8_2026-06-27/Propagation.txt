MODEL=opus-4.8  DATE=2026-06-27  (reviewed directly by orchestrator)

1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
Healthy, no actionable API-design findings. Six well-documented constants (REQUIRED, SUPPORTS, MANDATORY,
REQUIRES_NEW, NOT_SUPPORTED, NEVER) consumed by @Transactional; no methods. Naming mirrors the widely-known
Spring propagation vocabulary. (The omission of NESTED is a deliberate design choice — savepoint-based
nested transactions are not modeled — and is not flagged. Whether every constant is fully honored by the
runtime is an implementation-behavior question outside this public-API review.)
