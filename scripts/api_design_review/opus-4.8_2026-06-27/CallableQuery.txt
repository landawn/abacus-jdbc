API Design Review — CallableQuery.java
MODEL=opus-4.8  DATE=2026-06-27  (verified against source by orchestrator)

1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
CallableQuery extends AbstractQuery<CallableStatement, CallableQuery> and inherits the positional
setXxx + terminal-op API; its own additions are (a) a named setXxx(String,...) family that mirrors
NamedQuery, (b) the 12-overload registerOutParameter family, and (c) the *AndGetOutParameters terminal
ops. The bulk is healthy. registerOutParameter is a complete, faithful mirror of CallableStatement
across all three axes (index vs name; int sqlType vs SQLType; scale/typeName variants) with consistent
validation. The index-vs-name registerOutParameter(int,int) "two swappable ints" hazard is inherent to
the JDBC contract and already mitigated by the SQLType overloads (keep). The executeThenApply/Accept
additions and the query/list/queryAll/listAll...AndGetOutParameters ladder are sound. There are no
@Deprecated members and nothing is misplaced. Findings concentrate on real gaps where the named family
falls short of NamedQuery's mirror, plus minor inconsistencies in the multi-result-set ops.

2. NAMING ISSUES
- query2ResultSetsAndGetOutParameters / query3ResultSetsAndGetOutParameters | CallableQuery.java:2822,
  2913 | Low | The embedded digit is awkward but an established convention used consistently across the
  file's @see links; keep. Confirming check: grep the codebase for other *2ResultSets*/*3* names — the
  convention is referenced in the Javadoc @see tags here and in AbstractQuery.
- Otherwise the set/registerOutParameter/query/list/*AndGetOutParameters conventions are accurate and
  consistent.

3. RETURN-TYPE AND PARAMETER ISSUES
- setParameters(Object entity, List<String> parameterNames) | CallableQuery.java:1581 | Medium | VERIFIED.
  Uses List<String>, but the mirroring NamedQuery.setParameters(Object, Collection<String>) (NamedQuery
  4017) uses the wider Collection<String>. Recommend: widen to Collection<String> for consistency and to
  accept Set/other collections.
- queryAllResultSetsAndGetOutParameters / the listAllResultSetsAndGetOutParameters overloads |
  CallableQuery.java:2673, 2746, 3343, 3418, 3495, 3578, 3675 | Low | Unlike query2/query3...AndGet...
  (which explicitly call drainRemainingResultsForOutParams() before reading OUT params, 2849/2947), the
  "All" variants rely solely on iterateAllResultSets exhausting the iterator and never drain trailing
  update counts afterward — a potential stale-OUT-param risk on SQL Server/Oracle. Confirming check: read
  JdbcUtil.iterateAllResultSets to verify it advances past trailing update counts after the final result
  set; if not, add a drain call for parity. (This is a behavior/correctness check, not purely API.)

4. MISPLACED METHODS
- None. Every public method is intrinsic to a CallableStatement wrapper.

5. MISSING METHODS (with target class)
- setObject(String, Object, SQLType) and setObject(String, Object, SQLType, int scaleOrLength) |
  CallableQuery.java:1455-1506 (named setObject family ends at the int-sqlType form) | High | VERIFIED.
  The named setObject family stops at the int sqlType variants; NamedQuery provides both SQLType overloads
  (NamedQuery 3630, 3704) and CallableStatement natively supports them. Given the class already embraces
  SQLType throughout registerOutParameter, this is a real gap. Recommend: add both SQLType overloads
  (target: CallableQuery).
- setParameters(Object bean) single-arg | CallableQuery.java:1533 (Map), 1581 (Object,List) only | Low |
  VERIFIED: CallableQuery offers setParameters(Map) and setParameters(Object, List<String>) but not the
  single-arg bean overload setParameters(Object) that NamedQuery has (NamedQuery 3924). Recommend: add
  setParameters(Object) for mirror parity, unless procedures intentionally require explicit names.
- query2/query3ResultSetsAndGetOutParameters(ResultExtractor, ...) | CallableQuery.java:2822, 2913 |
  Medium | These accept only BiResultExtractor, whereas queryAndGetOutParameters and
  queryAllResultSetsAndGetOutParameters each offer both ResultExtractor and BiResultExtractor. (Same
  pattern as AbstractQuery.query2/query3ResultSets — cross-class; see SUMMARY.) Recommend: add the plain
  ResultExtractor overloads, or document the @Beta restriction.
- setNString(String, CharSequence) | CallableQuery.java:~734 (setNString family) | Low | NamedQuery offers
  this convenience (NamedQuery ~1323); CallableQuery does not. Recommend: add for a clean mirror.
  Confirming check: CallableStatement supports setNString(String,String) by name (it does). NOTE:
  setRef/setArray by name (present in NamedQuery) are correctly ABSENT here because CallableStatement has
  no name-based setRef/setArray — do NOT add those. The setInt(String, char/Character) pair is optional
  (and itself debatable).

6. DEPRECATION / REMOVAL CANDIDATES
- None. No existing @Deprecated members; no method warrants new deprecation. The two @Beta markers on
  query2/query3ResultSetsAndGetOutParameters (2821, 2912) are appropriate and should remain until the
  missing ResultExtractor overloads (section 5) are resolved.
