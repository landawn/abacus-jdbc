1. OVERALL ASSESSMENT
OnDeleteAction is an int-backed enum (NO_ACTION=0, SET_NULL=1, CASCADE=2). It was REALIGNED to the
IsolationLevel/FetchDirection convention: the backing int is exposed via intValue() (NOT value()) and the
reverse-lookup is valueOf(int) (NOT get(String)). The cross-enum accessor-naming inconsistency the original
report flagged NO LONGER EXISTS. The ENTIRE enum remains @Beta + @Deprecated (OnDeleteAction.java:29-30)
with guidance to model delete actions in the DB schema.
