A. Case-only / arity-only overload disambiguation (lambda-ambiguity workarounds) — STILL-VALID.
   AbstractQuery foreach vs forEach (8941/8995 vs 8696+); settParameters vs setParameters (3152/3199,
   class-doc added but not renamed); Jdbc HandlerFactory.create two single-arg overloads disambiguated only by
   lambda arity (6747/6779, still needs (TriConsumer)/(QuadConsumer) null casts to compile). Intent-unsafe.

   RowConsumer/BiRowConsumer.oneOff (Jdbc 4586/4628/4789/4831) still names the "wrap row into reusable
   DisposableObjArray" concept differently from RowMapper/BiRowMapper.toDisposableObjArray (2021+). The rename

   conspicuous. (Resolved by the same wave: ColumnGetter.forType (5751) and RowExtractor.forType (5159) now

   STILL-VALID in BOTH AbstractQuery (query2/query3ResultSets 5505/5571) and CallableQuery

   excluded-only 3-arg form (942+). DataTransferUtil exportCsv Writer side + DataSource cols gaps; importCsv
